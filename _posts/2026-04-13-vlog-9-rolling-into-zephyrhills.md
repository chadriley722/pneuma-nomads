another week on the road. another new spot.

we pulled into Encore Forest Lake Village in Zephyrhills and honestly — it was exactly what we needed. spacious sites, trees everywhere, that quiet that only exists when you're parked somewhere you've never been before.

## the travels

clermont to zephyrhills is a pretty easy haul. we've been stationary for a bit so there's always that mix of excitement and low-key chaos when it's time to hitch up and move. miley was losing her mind. lucy was unbothered. as usual.

## packing a jewelry order on the road

this is something people don't really see — the behind-the-scenes of running a small business out of an RV. no garage, no dedicated workspace, no extra storage. just a small table and whatever flat surface i can find.

someone placed an order on etsy and i filmed the whole packing process. bubble wrap, tissue paper, a handwritten note — i try to make every order feel like it was packed with intention, not just thrown in a box.

it's one of those things that makes [same old samsara](https://sameoldsamsara.xyz) feel real to me. someone out there is going to wear something i made with my hands, on the road, in an rv. that's kind of wild when i think about it.

## where we're headed next

still figuring it out. that's kind of the whole thing, isn't it.
