here it is. vlog one.

we've been living full-time in our RV for two years now — moving through forests, deserts, coastlines, and everything in between with our cat Lucy and our dog Miley. we never really documented it. until now.

this first video is a bit of everything. a fruit smoothie to start the day. a walk down to the lake. a run-in with some geese that got a little too confident. the usual pre-travel scramble for gas and road essentials. a deer on the side of the road doing deer things. and then a sunset that reminded us exactly why we do this.

we're parked at Thousand Trails Bay Landing in Bridgeport, TX — one of those spots that feels more like a neighborhood than a campground. it's home for now. tomorrow, we head east.

if you're new here — welcome. this is just us, documenting the life we've built on wheels. no scripts, no agenda. just the road and whatever it brings.
