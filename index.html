About Us

We’re Chad and Daniel, a nomadic couple living full-time in our RV. We left behind chasing the typical American dream—big job, big house, big car and a lifetime of “stuff”—so we could chase beautiful vistas and meaningful moments on the open road.

Along the way, we document everything from hidden waterfall hikes to cozy campfire evenings, weaving our love of adventure with ever-evolving creative pursuits. Fueled by curiosity everywhere we go—about remote landscapes and the nature of mind—each adventure is a canvas for new projects. We share our experiences, stories, and discoveries to spark inspiration in fellow wanderers, thinkers, and creators.

“Pneuma” means breath or spirit, and we believe each journey is an invitation to inhale wonder, exhale creativity, and awaken to life’s endless possibilities. Come wander with us.
